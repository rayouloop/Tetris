Small bug encountered with the collisions of the piece "|" which works 
only in pos 2 and 4, otherwise you have bugs and there will be holes under the piece when it will be placed.
Do not pay attention to warnings while compiling it does not affect the game in any kind ans does not disturb the gameplay.

Compilation                                     -> make

Execute                                         -> ./game

If you want to end the game without game over   -> CTRL+C

Welcome to CYTetris made by Zakaria Leshob and Rayen Mezni! In this game you will have the choice between 4 configuration of a random shape, 
and choose wisely the collumn you want to insert it into, the collomn refers to the left side of every shape.
Be careful not to get a piece out of the bounds of the grid, or your game will be over !
Have fun and thank you for playing CYTetris ! 
P.S.: to appreciate it at its best, play in fullscreen ;)
